AboutFlower of life

My name is Don Ruchira and I’d like to work with you to create the most magical, memorable and spectacular day, that you will forever hold very close to your heart.
I can bring you the support, expertise and experience that every couple needs, taking the stress out of the planning and allowing you to enjoy the countdown to your special day.
For just under 20 years I was thriving in the UK Travel industry, managing events with a specialism in décor, this was where my passion for weddings originated from and I only wanted to share this with others, like you.
The rest is history, as they say, and we have hosted some of the most prestigious weddings, with certifications from Wedding Academies, including the UK Alliance of Wedding Planners. I believe, with the expertise of myself and my trusted network of professional suppliers, we will create a day together that you’ll never forget.
So, that is my story…

My passion and love for flowers began as a young child helping my grandmother in her beautiful and enchanting flower garden. It is 2020, and after 30 years, I am blessed to continue my passion and love for creating Unique, Memorable, and Incomparable floral designs through my company Brandi Nicole Floral Designer. My studio is based out of my home in Temecula, California, where my client’s vision for their wedding day blossoms into an unforgettable reality.
As a floral designer, I pride myself in my artistry of floral design, and projecting a down to earth, honest, open, and consistent communication with my special clients. Providing my clients with exceptional personalized and stress-free service from start to finish. As the journey with my client’s blossoms, so do lasting friendships – each journey a treasure.
I strive to express my client’s vision by tirelessly seeking out unique materials and the most exceptional and varied blooms. I am a very detailed oriented floral designer – consistently keeping the client’s vision being executed with excellence.
I love my family, friends, nature, the beach, honesty, kindness, and positivity – this is what I, as a floral designer, am inspired by in life and floral design every day. My home reflects the Bohemian style, which I have fallen in love with for floral design, fashion, and home décor.
I would be honored to collaborate with you and provide an outstanding experience that blossoms your visions into reality on your wedding day!
